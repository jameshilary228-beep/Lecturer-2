What does this app do?
Converts lecturer voice to clean notes automatically.
Does it work on my phone?
Android (full features) + iPhone (screen must stay awake).

No. Offline mode works anywhere. Dictionary included offline.
Does it record when I lock my phone?
Android: Yes (screen off recording works). iPhone: Keep screen awake during lecture.
Does it write Pidgin or local language?
No. English only. Clean notes for exams.

Yes. Trained for Nigerian English accent.

App ignores it. Only lecturer voice enters notes.

Yes. Classmates share notes with one tap. Share button dey inside app.
